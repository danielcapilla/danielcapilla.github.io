Beyond The Trees

Beyond the Trees is my first project on Blue Rainfrog Studios. It's a Rogue-like game where you, as a knight, decide to go into the forest looking for an adventure to try to find the famous treasure that is inside.

What I did: GUI programming. Gameplay programming such as movement and shooting mechanic, currency, win/lose conditions and bug fixes. Enemies Behaviour (Squirrels with a Beahaviour Tree) and Database management.

What I used: Unity (C#), Visual Studio, Photoshop, Github and Itch.io.

You can play it for free here: Beyond The Trees

Multiplayer Fighting Game

A multiplayer 2D fighting game developed with Unity and Netcode for GameObjects, supporting up to 5 players and featuring 3 unique playable characters. Inspired by games such as Super Smash Bros and Brawlhalla.

What I did:Converted the game from single-player to multiplayer using Netcode tools. Handled connections and disconnections at different stages of the game. Implemented an authoritative host/server architecture and added a chat feature in the lobby.

What I used: Unity (C#), Visual Studio, Netcode for GameObjects, Github.

Froggy Ware

Froggy Ware is a video game that imitates the Nintendo WarioWare series, featuring fast-paced and frenetic mini-games designed to keep the player entertained. These mini-games utilize mobile sensors, such as the gyroscope and accelerometer.

What I did:Developed a Unity game that uses mobile sensors like the gyroscope and accelerometer, implementing different design patterns such as the pool and observer.

What I used: Unity (C#), Visual Studio, Unity Remote 5, Github.
